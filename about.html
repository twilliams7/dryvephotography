PORSCHE GALLERY- SPECS AND MODS

The Porsche Gallery is a collection of photographs of our 2003 Porsche 986 Boxster S. The pristine guards red paint and 18" Carrera Wheels make for some stunning photos.

    Options, Specs and Mods-

  • Guards Red Paint
  • 18” Factory Carrera Alloys which look absolutely amazing.
  • Colored Porsche logo center wheel caps, Porsche logo on shift handle, more Porsche crap, etc.
  • Bose premium audio system
  • All-aluminum look interior (steering wheel, center console, shift knob, parking brake, door handles, etc.) and it has the most factory aluminum finish I've ever seen in a 986.
  • Don’t think it is an option, but metropol blue (navy) top and interior. Super hard to find red/navy from my experience.
  • Running Michelin Pilot Super Sports right now, car came with Eagle F1s but they were cupping.
  • Factory Sport Kit bumpers and lips
  • Brembo brakes with red colored Porsche calipers
  • De-ambered the front and rear lights. Plan to add Macan style LED projectors to the headlights this summer. (DONE!)
  • Hella smoked side markers
  • Porsche Performance Sport Exhaust System (PSE) which sounds and looks absolutely amazing… The best burble ever from a six cylinder.
  • Replaced front and rear control arms (because of a rattle from the back, but car also drives much stiffer and “handles like it’s on rails”, as I always say.)
  • Replaced rear wheel bearings because of the same rattle. Rattle is gone!
  • It had an aftermarket 12 disc CD changer, but it's now obsolete... so we took it out for a little more trunk space.
  • Added an integrated Bluetooth receiver into the car to use with your phone. Not hard to do, but easy to use. Also added a small microphone and and call button to make it act like a real car system.
